Beyond the Standard:
A Virtual Guide to English Accents

An interactive resource for EFL learners to explore the phonetic landscapes of World Englishes. Step beyond a single standard and discover the richness of global accent variation.

Rationale & Purpose

Most learners equate "good English" with a single standard. This project challenges that assumption, providing tools to critically evaluate and appreciate accent variation throughout the world.

Our interactive resource aims to make linguistic data and pedagogical value accessible to learners of English at different levels. It is paving the way for global communicative competence.
