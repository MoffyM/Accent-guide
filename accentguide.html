Beyond the Standard:
A Virtual Guide to English Accents

An interactive resource for EFL learners to explore the phonetic landscapes of World Englishes. Step beyond a single standard and discover the richness of global accent variation.

Rationale & Purpose

Most learners equate "good English" with a single standard. This project challenges that assumption, providing tools to critically evaluate and appreciate accent variation across the globe.

Our interactive resource bridges the gap between linguistic data and pedagogical value, fostering global communicative competence for learners at every level.
